# Role Onboarding & Handoff Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure a smooth onboarding or transition for key project roles. Adapt as needed for each role.

## Before Onboarding/Handoff

- [ ] Role responsibilities are clearly documented in project documentation
- [ ] Success criteria and expected outcomes are communicated
- [ ] Key contacts and stakeholders identified
- [ ] Access to required tools, systems, and documentation is provisioned
- [ ] Upcoming milestones and priorities reviewed

## During Onboarding/Handoff

- [ ] Knowledge transfer sessions scheduled/attended
- [ ] Pending tasks and outstanding issues reviewed
- [ ] Role-specific processes, checklists, and templates explained
- [ ] Shadowing or mentorship arranged (if applicable)

## After Onboarding/Handoff

- [ ] New role-holder confirms understanding and readiness
- [ ] Stakeholders introduced and included in communications
- [ ] Feedback and improvement suggestions collected from outgoing role-holder (if applicable)
